BNB Bits are a set of 2,500 collectable 8-bit character NFTs on Binance Smart Chain. BNB Bits went live for sale at the rate of 1 BNB per BNB Bit NFT. BNB Bits is a community project, not affiliated with Binance or Binance Smart Chain.

BNB Bits are generated pixelart GIFs and still animations, all 2,500 randomly generated with a random characteristic set. Rare traits are yet to be found, and it is all up to what the generator produced for these 2,500. People will discover rare traits over time as they analyze and review the BNB Bits pixel art images.

The only scarcity implemented from the genesis is 10 randomly placed and generated BNB Bits with a gold background, and the final 8 BNB Bits have a rainbow background.
